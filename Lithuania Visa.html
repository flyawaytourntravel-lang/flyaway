About Lithuania

Lithuania is a beautiful Baltic country known for its medieval castles, lush forests, and vibrant culture. Vilnius, the capital, boasts a UNESCO World Heritage Old Town and a welcoming atmosphere for visitors.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with minimum 3 months validity beyond stay
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photos meeting Schengen standards
  • Travel insurance with minimum €30,000 medical coverage
  • Proof of accommodation and detailed travel itinerary
  • Proof of financial means for the duration of stay
  • Round-trip flight booking
  • Employment or study documents if applicable

Processing Time

Visa processing typically takes 10-15 business days. Early application is advised to ensure timely approval.

Types of Visa

  • Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For tourism, business, or family visits up to 90 days.
  • National Visa (Type D): For longer stays, such as work or study.
  • Student Visa: For academic purposes.
  • Work Visa: For employment-related stays.

Key Information on the Austrian Job Visa

Austria offers a straightforward path for skilled workers to secure a professional job through the **Red-White-Red Card** program. This visa is designed to attract qualified professionals from outside the EU and provides a flexible permit for work and residency.

Eligibility & Benefits

  • A points-based system considers your qualifications, work experience, age, language skills, and job offer.
  • The Red-White-Red Card is valid for 24 months and can be renewed if you continue to meet the criteria.
  • Offers a path to permanent residency in Austria.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Detailed CV/Resume outlining professional experience
  • Proof of academic qualifications (degrees, diplomas)
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to support your stay
  • Valid travel health insurance

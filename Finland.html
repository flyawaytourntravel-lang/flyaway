What is the Finland Jobseeker Visa?

The Finland Jobseeker Visa allows qualified professionals from non-EU countries to travel to Finland for up to six months to search for a job. This visa is designed for individuals who have the skills and qualifications that are in demand in Finland ’s labour market but don’t yet have a concrete job offer.

Who Can Apply?

  • Qualifications: Recognized university degree or equivalent qualification.
  • Work Experience: Relevant professional experience in your field.
  • Financial Stability: Proof of sufficient funds to support yourself.
  • Language Skills: German is not mandatory but preferred.

Living Expenses and Lifestyle

  • Cost of Living: €700–€1,200/month
  • Accommodation: €800–€1,500/month
  • Health Insurance: Mandatory
  • Transport: €70–€100/month

Processing Time

4 to 12 weeks depending on completeness and visa office.

Visa Fees

Application Fee: €75
Proof of Funds: ~€6,000 for 6 months

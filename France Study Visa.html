Top French Universities & Grandes Écoles

  • Sorbonne University – Historic and prestigious in Paris
  • Université PSL – Known for innovation and academic excellence
  • University of Grenoble Alpes – Strong in tech, business, and research
  • HEC Paris – Top-ranked business school in Europe
  • École Normale Supérieure – Elite training in humanities and sciences

Tuition Fees

  • Public Universities (UG): €170 – €450/year
  • Grandes Écoles: €6,000 – €15,000/year
  • Private Institutions: €4,000 – €25,000/year

Living Expenses

Monthly cost of living: €800 – €1,200 including accommodation, food, transport, and essentials.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

  • Eiffel Excellence Scholarship: Covers tuition + living costs
  • Charpak Scholarship: For Indian students
  • Erasmus Mundus: For joint master's across Europe

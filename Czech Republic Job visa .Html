Key Information on the Czech Republic Job Visa

The Czech Republic offers a high quality of life with a strong industrial and technological base. For non-EU citizens, the most common work permit is the **Employee Card**, which allows you to work and reside in the country for a period of up to two years.

Eligibility & Benefits

  • The Employee Card allows you to work and reside in the Czech Republic for an initial period of up to two years.
  • Eligibility is based on having a valid job offer from a Czech employer and meeting the required professional qualifications.
  • This unified permit simplifies the process, as it combines both the work and residence permits into a single document.

Required Documents

To apply for an Employee Card, you will generally need the following documents:

  • Valid passport with sufficient validity.
  • Completed and signed visa application form.
  • Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Employment contract or a letter of intent from a Czech employer.
  • Proof of accommodation in the Czech Republic (e.g., rental agreement).
  • Detailed CV or resume.
  • Proof of academic qualifications (degrees, diplomas).
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to support your stay.
  • Valid travel health insurance.

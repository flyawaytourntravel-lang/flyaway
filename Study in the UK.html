UK Study Visa: An Overview

  • Choose Your Course and University: Secure a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS).
  • Visa Requirements:
    • Unconditional offer from a UK institution
    • Sufficient financial support
    • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Complete Visa Application:
    • Valid passport
    • Proof of CAS & funds
    • Language test results
    • Visa fee (~£348)
  • Biometrics Appointment + Decision (up to 3 weeks)
  • Visa Validity: Course duration + additional time

Admission Process

  • Research & Select Course
  • Meet Entry Requirements (Academics, IELTS, etc.)
  • Apply:
    • UG: UCAS (up to 5 choices)
    • PG: Direct to university
  • Interview (if required) → Accept Offer → Apply for Visa (with CAS)

Cost of Living

  • London: £1,200–£1,800/month
  • Outside London: £900–£1,300/month
  • Includes housing, food, transport, leisure
  • Work part-time (up to 20 hrs/week)

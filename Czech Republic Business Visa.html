Czech Republic Business Visa Overview

The Czech Republic is a key business hub in Central Europe, known for its strong manufacturing base, growing tech sector, and strategic location. Business visas are ideal for attending meetings, negotiations, trade fairs, or forming partnerships.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Recent passport-size photos
  • Invitation letter from a Czech company
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel insurance covering Schengen area
  • Accommodation details

Visa Processing Time

Usually processed within 15 calendar days, depending on the applicant's case and season.

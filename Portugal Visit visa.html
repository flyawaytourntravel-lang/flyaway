Portugal Visa Overview

Portugal is famous for its stunning beaches, historic cities, delicious cuisine, and warm climate. Whether you plan to explore Lisbon’s charming streets or relax on the Algarve coast, Portugal offers a diverse and welcoming travel experience.

Documents Required

  • Valid Passport (issued within last 10 years, valid for at least 3 months beyond stay)
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (as per Schengen requirements)
  • Cover letter stating purpose of visit and itinerary
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings or invitation letter)
  • Travel insurance (minimum €30,000 coverage)
  • Proof of financial means (bank statements, income tax returns)
  • Flight reservation or travel itinerary
  • Employment proof or student ID if applicable

Visa Processing Time

Processing usually takes 10 to 15 business days. It’s best to apply 3-4 weeks before your intended travel.

Types of Visa

  • Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For stays up to 90 days for tourism, business, or family visits.
  • Long-Stay Visa (Type D): For longer stays such as work, study, or family reunification.
  • Student Visa: For academic programs in Portugal.
  • Work Visa: For employment in Portugal with a valid job offer.

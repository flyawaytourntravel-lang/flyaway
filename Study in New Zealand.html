New Zealand is known for its breathtaking landscapes, friendly people, and high-quality education system. If you’re planning to study here, obtaining a Student Visa is your first step toward achieving your academic dreams.

Why Choose New Zealand?

  • World-Class Education: Internationally recognized universities and degrees.
  • Cultural Diversity: A safe, welcoming, and multicultural environment.
  • Work While You Study: Part-time work rights during studies and full-time during breaks.
  • Post-Study Opportunities: Various work visas after graduation to gain experience.

Types of Student Visas

  • Fee-Paying Student Visa: For full-time study in a recognized course.
  • Pathway Student Visa: For studying multiple courses leading to higher qualifications.
  • Visitor Visa for Study: For short courses (less than 3 months).

Key Eligibility Requirements

  • Offer from a recognized New Zealand institution
  • Proof of sufficient funds for tuition & living costs
  • Health & Character clearance (medical & police check)
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL if required)
  • Genuine intention to study

Application Process

  • Gather required documents (enrolment proof, funds, insurance, passport, language test results)
  • Submit application online or at a Visa Application Centre
  • Attend interview if requested
  • Wait for decision (2–8 weeks typical)
  • Receive visa and prepare for departure

Visa Fees

  • Application Fee: NZD 330–500
  • Medical Examination: NZD 100–250 (if required)
  • Police Clearance: Varies by country

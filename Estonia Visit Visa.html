About Estonia

Estonia combines stunning nature with cutting-edge digital infrastructure, offering visitors a unique blend of history and modernity. From Tallinn’s UNESCO-listed Old Town to the serene forests and lakes, Estonia is an enchanting destination.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with at least 3 months validity beyond your stay
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Recent passport photos meeting Schengen specifications
  • Travel insurance covering medical emergencies with minimum €30,000
  • Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
  • Proof of sufficient financial means
  • Round-trip flight reservation
  • Employment or student documents if applicable

Processing Time

Visa applications typically take 10-15 business days. It is recommended to apply well in advance to avoid delays.

Types of Visa

  • Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For tourism, business, or family visits up to 90 days.
  • National Visa (Type D): For long-term stays such as work or study.
  • Student Visa: For academic studies in Estonia.
  • Work Visa: For employment purposes.

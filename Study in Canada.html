Canada Student Visa: Basic Eligibility

  • Proof of Acceptance: Acceptance letter from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI)
  • Financial Resources: Proof of CAD 10,000+ for tuition, living, and travel (more for dependents)
  • No Criminal Record: Police certificate required
  • Health Requirements: Medical exam (if applicable)
  • Intent to Leave: Show ties to home country (job, family, assets)
  • Language Proficiency: IELTS, TOEFL, or TEF as needed

Required Documents

  • Valid Passport (6+ months beyond stay)
  • Letter of Acceptance (DLI)
  • Proof of Funds (bank statements, affidavits)
  • Passport-size Photos (per Canadian specs)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • Medical Exam Results (if required)
  • IELTS/TOEFL/TEF Scores
  • Academic Transcripts & Certificates
  • Proof of Relationship & Funds (if dependents)
  • Completed Visa Forms (IMM 1294) & Fee Payment
  • Biometrics (if applicable)

Application Steps

  • Gather All Documents
  • Complete IMM 1294 Application
  • Submit Online or via VAC
  • Attend Biometrics Appointment (if needed)
  • Wait for Visa Processing
  • Receive Visa + Letter of Introduction

About Slovakia

Slovakia boasts stunning natural scenery including the Tatra Mountains, medieval castles like Spiš, and a rich cultural heritage. It is a Schengen Area member state, making it easy for travelers to explore multiple European destinations.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with minimum 3 months validity beyond stay
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Passport-size photos meeting Schengen requirements
  • Travel insurance with minimum €30,000 medical coverage
  • Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
  • Proof of sufficient financial means
  • Round-trip flight bookings
  • Proof of employment or studies if applicable

Processing Time

Visa applications typically take 10-15 business days. Early application is recommended.

Types of Visa

  • Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For tourism, business, or family visits up to 90 days.
  • National Visa (Type D): For long stays such as work or study.
  • Student Visa: For academic programs in Slovakia.
  • Work Visa: For employment opportunities.

Free Education in Germany – Eligibility & Benefits

  • Eligibility:
    • Accepted into a public university
    • Meet German visa requirements
    • German proficiency (B1/B2 or English for specific programs)
    • Proof of funds (~€11,208/year)
  • Benefits:
    • No tuition fees
    • High-quality education
    • Excellent career prospects
    • Cultural and research hub

Paid Education – Eligibility & Advantages

  • Eligibility:

  • Advantages:

    • Smaller class sizes
    • International recognition
    • Faster degree completion

Tuition Fees and Living Costs

  • Public Universities: €250–€350/semester (admin fees)
  • Private Universities: €5,000–€20,000/year
  • Living Costs: €800–€1,200/month

Germany Study Visa Process from India

  • Get accepted by a German university
  • Collect required documents:
    • Valid passport
    • Admission letter
    • Proof of funds
    • Health insurance
    • Transcripts & language test scores
  • Apply at German Embassy/Consulate in India
  • Visa processing: 6–8 weeks
  • Attend visa interview
  • Visa Fees: €75–€100 (~₹7,000–₹9,000)

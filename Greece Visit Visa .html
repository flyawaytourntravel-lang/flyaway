About Greece

Greece is famed for its rich archaeological heritage, vibrant culture, and stunning Mediterranean coastline. It is part of the Schengen Area, allowing visa holders to travel freely across many European countries.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with at least 3 months validity beyond your stay
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Passport-sized photos meeting Schengen specifications
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation proof
  • Travel insurance with minimum €30,000 coverage
  • Proof of financial means
  • Flight reservation
  • Employment or student status proof

Processing Time

Typically 10-15 working days. It’s recommended to apply 3-4 weeks before your planned travel.

Visa Types

  • Schengen Short-Stay (Type C): Up to 90 days for tourism, business, or family visits.
  • Long-Stay Visa (Type D): For longer stays like study or work in Greece.
  • Student Visa: For academic courses within Greece.
  • Work Visa: For employment with a valid job offer in Greece.

Sweden Jobseeker Visa

Looking to jumpstart your career in one of the most innovative and thriving economies in Europe? Sweden, with its high quality of life, strong job market, and excellent work-life balance, is an ideal destination for skilled professionals. The Sweden Jobseeker Visa allows you to enter the country to search for employment. Here’s everything you need to know:

What is the Sweden Jobseeker Visa?

The Sweden Jobseeker Visa is designed for individuals who wish to come to Sweden and search for a job. You're allowed to stay in Sweden for up to six months and apply for a work permit once hired.

Key Eligibility Criteria

  • Citizen of a non-EU/EEA country
  • Sufficient funds for your stay
  • Proof of qualifications and skills
  • Health insurance for your stay

Application Process

  • Gather documents (passport, proof of funds, insurance, etc.)
  • Apply online or at a Swedish embassy
  • Wait 2–4 weeks for processing
  • If approved, start planning your trip!

Living Costs

  • Rent: SEK 10,000–15,000/month
  • Groceries: SEK 2,000–3,500/month
  • Utilities: SEK 800–1,500/month
  • Transport: SEK 900–1,200/month

Sweden Lifestyle

  • Work-life balance: flexible work culture
  • Outdoor activities: hiking, skiing, cycling
  • Social benefits: healthcare, education
  • Culture: museums, sustainability, arts

USA Study Visa Types

To study in the USA, you’ll need a student visa. The most common is the F-1 Visa. For vocational schools, the M-1 Visa may apply.

Key Costs for Studying in the USA

  • Undergraduate: $20,000–$50,000/year
  • Graduate: $25,000–$70,000/year
  • Community College: $8,000–$15,000/year
  • Living Expenses: $6,000–$20,000/year

Processing Time

Steps: SEVP Approval → I-20 Form → Visa Interview → Approval. Start early!

Visa Fees

  • DS-160 Application Fee: $160
  • SEVIS Fee: $350
  • Visa Issuance Fee: $0–$200 (varies by nationality)

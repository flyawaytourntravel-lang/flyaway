Switzerland Business Visa Overview

Switzerland is a leading destination for international business due to its stable economy, cutting-edge research, and strategic location in Europe. The business visa enables travelers to attend meetings, conferences, or explore partnerships.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Invitation letter from Swiss company or partner
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel insurance (Schengen compliant)
  • Proof of accommodation

About Luxembourg

Luxembourg is a small, wealthy country nestled between Belgium, France, and Germany. It is known for its medieval old town, dense Ardennes forest, and as a key financial center in Europe.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport with minimum 3 months validity beyond stay
  • Completed Schengen visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photos following Schengen requirements
  • Travel insurance with minimum €30,000 medical coverage
  • Proof of accommodation and detailed travel itinerary
  • Proof of sufficient financial means
  • Round-trip flight reservation
  • Employment or academic documents if applicable

Processing Time

Visa applications typically process within 10 to 15 business days. Early application is advised.

Types of Visa

  • Schengen Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For tourism, family visits, or short business stays up to 90 days.
  • National Visa (Type D): For longer stays such as work or study purposes.
  • Student Visa: For academic studies.
  • Work Visa: For employment.

Norway Business Visa Overview

Norway is a powerhouse in maritime trade, energy production, and technology development. A business visa allows foreign nationals to attend corporate meetings, conferences, exhibitions, or explore partnership opportunities.

Documents Required

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed and signed visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Invitation letter from a Norwegian business partner
  • Proof of accommodation in Norway
  • Evidence of financial means
  • Travel insurance covering the Schengen area

Visa Processing Time

Processing generally takes 10–15 working days, but can vary depending on the time of year and application complexity.

Fee Structure

Undergraduate:

  • Tuition: R40,000 – R100,000/year
  • Living: R60,000 – R100,000/year
  • Accommodation: R50,000 – R70,000/year

Postgraduate:

  • Tuition: R50,000 – R120,000/year
  • Living: R70,000 – R100,000/year
  • Accommodation: R55,000 – R80,000/year

Additional Costs: Books (R5,000 – R10,000), Health Insurance (R10,000 – R15,000)

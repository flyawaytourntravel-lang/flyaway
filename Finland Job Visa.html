Key Information on the Finland Job Visa

Finland is known for its innovation, high quality of life, and strong demand for skilled professionals. The most common route for non-EU citizens is applying for a **Residence Permit for Employment**, which allows you to live and work in Finland legally.

Eligibility & Benefits

  • The residence permit allows you to stay and work in Finland as long as your employment continues.
  • You must have a confirmed job offer from a Finnish employer.
  • Possibility to apply for a permanent residence permit after continuous stay.
  • Finland offers excellent healthcare, education, and work-life balance.

Required Documents

While requirements may vary, the following are generally needed:

  • Valid passport with sufficient validity.
  • Completed and signed residence permit application form.
  • Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Employment contract or binding job offer from a Finnish company.
  • Proof of accommodation in Finland.
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to support your stay.
  • Valid travel health insurance covering your entire stay.

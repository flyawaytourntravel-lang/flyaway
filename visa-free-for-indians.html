This list is taken from consolidated reporting (Henley Passport Index summaries and news outlets in July 2025) and groups destinations by how they typically allow Indian passport holders to enter without a prior embassy visa. Some destinations have special rules (e.g., longer stays if you hold a valid US/Schengen/UK visa, or temporary visa-free windows) — check the destination authority for the exact requirements and permitted length of stay before booking.

How to use this list

1) Treat each country’s entry rules as subject to change. 2) "Visa-free" means you typically do not need to apply in advance. 3) "Visa-on-arrival" means a visa is issued when you land (may require documentation/fees). 4) "ETA" requires a small electronic pre-registration in some cases. 5) Always confirm with the destination embassy or official immigration website.

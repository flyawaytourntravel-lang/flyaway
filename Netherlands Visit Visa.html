Netherlands Visa Overview

The Netherlands is a welcoming country, famous for its extensive canal systems, historic architecture, and thriving arts scene. It attracts millions of tourists and professionals annually. Whether visiting for tourism, business, study, or work, obtaining the correct visa is essential.

Documents Required

  • Valid Passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed and signed visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (as per guidelines)
  • Proof of accommodation in the Netherlands
  • Detailed travel itinerary
  • Proof of financial means to support your stay
  • Travel insurance with medical coverage
  • Letter of invitation (if applicable)

Visa Processing Time

Applications are generally processed within 10 to 15 business days. Early application is recommended, especially during peak travel seasons.

Types of Visa

  • Short-Stay Schengen Visa (Type C): For tourism, business trips, or family visits up to 90 days.
  • Long-Stay Visa (MVV): For studies, work, or family reunification lasting longer than 90 days.
  • Student Visa: For individuals accepted into recognized educational institutions.
  • Work Visa: For professionals with employment offers in the Netherlands.
